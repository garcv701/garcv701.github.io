The Honorific Titles of Former President Bush

HOGAN

John McCain

Politician

Arizona Senator

Born: 08-29-1936
Hometown: Coco Solo,
Panama Canal Zone
Education: United States
Naval Academy

This title is a reference to the American sitcom Hogan's Heroes which was set in a German prisoner of war camp. Bush thought this was appropriate because McCain was an actual prisoner of war in Vietnam.
