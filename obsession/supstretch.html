The Honorific Titles of Former President Bush

SUPER STRETCH

Bill Sammmon

Journalist

Former White House
Correspondent

Born: 1959
Hometown: Cleveland, Ohio
Education: Miami University

Standing at a towering 6 feet 7 inches, Sammon earned the name Super Stretch. He is taller than Little Stretch and Stretch, two other journalists also notable enough for their height to also earn nicknames.
