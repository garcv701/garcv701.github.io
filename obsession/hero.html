Colin Powell was a four-star general in the U.S. Army, and has had major involvement in the government. He is a liberal Republican who contested many actions of the Bush administration. Although he was politcially untouchable he recieved scorn from many in Washington for his efforts to communicate the situation in Iraq to the U.N. Bush's nickname displays the feelings of admiration, as well as resentment within his administration for Powell.
