Beatnik is a term referring to someone of the Beat Generation literary movement of the 50s and 60s. Elements of Beat culture include spirituality, sexual freedom, rejection of materialism, and use of psychadelic drugs. Doug Wead being a conservative republican does not hold the values of Beat culture at all, thus Weadnik is a name given for the irony.
