The Honorific Titles of Former President Bush

TURD BLOSSOM

Karl Rove

Staff

All Star

Former Senior Advisor

Born: 12-25-1950
Hometown: Denver, Colorado
Education: University of Utah,
University of Maryland

A "turd blossom" is a Texan term for a flower that grows from a pile of cow feces. Former President Bush has not stated why he gave Karl Rove the nickname, but looking at his career he has been known to use divisive fearmongering tactics to secure campaign victories. Turd Blossom can be seen as a metaphor for sprouting victory using detrimental strategies.
